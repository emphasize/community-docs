Where to get OVOS?

OVOS is in early stages, we publish our raspberry pi images for download but expect new bugs and new fixes on every release, we are not yet stable!

These images are development images in alpha stage, bugs and incomplete features are guaranteed

  • SSH Details for Mark-2/DevKit Image: Username: ovos | password: ovos

  • manjaro

Flashing your image

Flashing your image to your sdcard or USB drive is not different than flashing any other image. For the non-technical users we advise to use the flashing utility from the Raspberry Pi Foundation which you can find here. Under "CHOOSE OS" select custom at the very bottom of the list and browse to the downloaded image file. It is not required to unzip / unpack the image as the Raspberry Pi imager software can do that for you on the fly. Under "CHOOSE STORAGE" select your sdcard or USB device.

If you have a Raspberri Pi 4 we recommend to use a good USB3.1 device. If you have a Raspberry Pi 3, use a proper sdcard. (From fast to slow: USB3.1 - sdcard - USB2)
